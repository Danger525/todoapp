About My To-Do List

A simple and efficient task management application.

What is My To-Do List?

My To-Do List is a simple, user-friendly application designed to help users organize their daily tasks efficiently. It allows users to **add, update, and delete tasks** with a clean and interactive interface.

✔ Easy Task Management

Add, edit, and remove tasks seamlessly with a simple interface.

✔ Fully Responsive

The design works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

✔ Secure & Fast

Built with Flask and SQLite, ensuring quick and efficient performance.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQLite
  • Frameworks & Libraries: Flask-SQLAlchemy
